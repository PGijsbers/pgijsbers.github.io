Pieter Gijsbers

Hi! I am a research engineer at Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e) in Joaquin Vanschoren's automated machine learning (AutoML) lab. I support (automated) machine learning research through open source projects like OpenML and the AutoML Benchmark.


I have been a long-time open source developer, starting early 2016 with my first contribution to OpenML. While working on my thesis on Systems for AutoML Research, I continued and expanded this by creating AutoML framework GAMA, starting the AutoML Benchmark project, and bringing many new improvements to openml-python including support for benchmarking suites.

Built by me, open source.
